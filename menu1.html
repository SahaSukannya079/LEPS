Every word has a story to tell, an image to portray and a sound to evoke.
LEPS Education advances this philosophy, presenting a visually spelled out method to learn vocabulary.
A strong advocate of diversity and inclusion, LEPS (LEarning through Pictures and Sounds) harnesses the entire set of crayons, paving the path for building a visual dictionary in English and Bangla to picking up words from the languages of indigenous communities that hold historical or cultural importance.
LEPS believes that every language deserves to flow like an endless river.
